Common Questions About Cash Offers

Is there really no cost to me?

Correct. We cover all closing costs, there are no realtor commissions, and no hidden fees. The offer you accept is what you receive.

How fast can we close?

We can close in as little as 7 days, or on your timeline if you need more time. We work around your schedule.

Do I need to make repairs?

No repairs needed. We buy homes in any condition - even if they need major work. Just leave everything as-is.

What if I'm behind on payments?

That's okay. We work with homeowners in foreclosure regularly. We can negotiate with your lender and handle all the details.

How is the offer calculated?

We base our offers on comparable sales, property condition, and current market conditions. We aim to make fair offers that work for both parties.

Am I obligated if I get an offer?

Absolutely not. Our offers are completely no-obligation. You can take time to think it over or simply decline - no pressure.
