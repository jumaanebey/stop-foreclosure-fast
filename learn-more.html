Your Options Explained

Loan Modification

We negotiate with your lender to modify your loan terms - potentially lowering your interest rate, extending your term, or reducing principal. This can make your payments affordable again and let you keep your home.

Best for: Homeowners who want to stay and can afford reduced payments

Forbearance Agreement

A temporary pause or reduction in payments while you get back on your feet. This buys you time to recover from a financial setback without losing your home.

Best for: Temporary hardship situations (job loss, medical emergency)

Quick Sale

Sell your home before the auction to preserve your credit and walk away with your equity. We connect you with vetted cash buyers who can close quickly - often in 7-14 days.

Best for: Homeowners with equity who need to move on

Short Sale

If you owe more than your home is worth, we can negotiate with your lender to accept less than the full balance. This lets you avoid foreclosure and its severe credit impact.

Best for: Underwater homeowners who need a clean exit
