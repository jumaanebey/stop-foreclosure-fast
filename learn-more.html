Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you stop my foreclosure? +

In most cases, we can stop or postpone a foreclosure within 24-72 hours. If your sale is within 7 days, we work even faster with emergency procedures. The key is contacting us as soon as possible.

How much does your service cost? +

Our consultation is completely free. If we buy your house, there are no fees or commissions - we pay all closing costs. If we help with loan modification, our fee is typically paid by the lender, not you.

Can you help if I'm already in foreclosure? +

Yes! We specialize in urgent cases. Whether you received a Notice of Default, Notice of Sale, or your auction is days away, we can help. We've stopped foreclosures scheduled for the next day.

Will this affect my credit score? +

A completed foreclosure can drop your credit score by 200-300 points and stay on your report for 7 years. By avoiding foreclosure through our solutions, you minimize credit damage and can rebuild much faster.

Do I have to leave my home? +

Not necessarily. If we can arrange a loan modification or repayment plan, you keep your home. If selling is the best option, we give you time to relocate properly - typically 30-60 days with moving assistance.
