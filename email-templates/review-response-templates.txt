RESPONSE BEST PRACTICES:
========================
1. Respond within 24-48 hours
2. Always be professional and empathetic
3. Thank them for their feedback (even if negative)
4. Don't get defensive with negative reviews
5. Take negative discussions offline when possible
6. Personalize responses - don't copy/paste exactly
7. Include your business name for SEO
8. Keep responses concise but genuine
