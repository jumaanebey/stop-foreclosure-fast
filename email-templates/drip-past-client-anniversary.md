# Past Client Anniversary Email Sequence
## Long-Term Relationship Building & Referral Generation

---

## Email 1: 6-Month Check-In

**Send Timing:** 6 months after closing date

**Subject Line Options:**
- 6 months later - how's the new chapter going?
- Thinking of you, {{first_name}}
- Half a year since your fresh start

**Preview Text:** Just checking in to see how you're doing.

**Body:**

Hi {{first_name}},

Can you believe it's already been 6 months since we closed on {{property_address}}?

I've been thinking about you and wanted to check in. How are things going?

---

**I hope the past 6 months have brought you:**

- Peace of mind knowing the property situation is behind you
- Space to focus on what matters most to you
- A chance to breathe and plan for the future

---

When we work with homeowners, we know we're helping them through one of the most stressful times in their lives. That's why it means so much to us to hear how things turn out afterward.

**If you have a moment, I'd love to hear:**
- How are you settling into this new chapter?
- Is there anything you wish you'd known that we could share with future clients?
- Any feedback on your experience working with us?

Just hit reply - I read every response personally.

---

**Still Have Questions?**

Sometimes things come up months after a sale - tax questions, mail forwarding issues, or just general real estate questions. If you need anything, don't hesitate to reach out. We're still here for you.

**CTA Button:** Reply With an Update

Wishing you continued peace and prosperity,

{{sender_name}}
{{company_name}}
{{phone_number}}

P.S. If you know anyone who's currently struggling with their property situation, we'd be honored to help them the same way we helped you. Just send them our way.

## Sequence Notes

**Automation Triggers:**
- Set recurring annual reminder based on closing date
- 6-month check-in: One-time send
- 1-year anniversary: One-time send
- Annual check-in: Repeat yearly (Year 2, Year 3, Year 4, etc.)
- Remove from sequence only if client requests or email bounces

**Personalization Fields Required:**
- {{first_name}}
- {{property_address}}
- {{their_situation_brief}} (from CRM notes)
- {{years}} (calculated from closing date)
- {{sender_name}}
- {{company_name}}
- {{phone_number}}
- {{testimonial_link}}

**Anniversary Gift Ideas (1-Year):**
- $25-50 gift card (Amazon, Visa, restaurant)
- Donation to charity of their choice
- Personalized item (card, small gift)
- Local business gift certificate

**Gift Logistics:**
- Send within 1 week of email open
- Track gift delivery confirmation
- Follow up with quick thank-you if they post a review

**Long-Term Relationship Value:**
- Past clients are your best referral source
- Consistent annual contact keeps you top-of-mind
- Referrals from past clients have highest conversion rate
- These relationships compound over time

**CRM Integration:**
- Log all anniversary emails sent
- Track responses and engagement
- Note any life updates shared by client
- Flag high-engagement past clients as "referral partners"

**A/B Testing Suggestions:**
- Test gift offers vs. no gift offers
- Test referral ask in every email vs. alternating emails
- Test personal vs. company-branded messaging

**Compliance Reminders:**
- Include unsubscribe link in all emails
- Allow opt-out from marketing while maintaining transactional communications
- Ensure gift offers comply with local regulations
