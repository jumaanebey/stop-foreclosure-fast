Virtual Consultation FAQ

How long does a virtual consultation take?

Most consultations take 30-45 minutes. We'll take as much time as needed to thoroughly review your situation and answer all your questions.

What if I'm not comfortable with video calls?

No problem! We can do phone-only consultations. Many clients prefer this option and it's equally effective for reviewing your situation.

Can you really help if you haven't seen my property?

Absolutely. Most foreclosure solutions depend on financial analysis, timeline, and legal strategy - not physical property inspection. We can provide accurate cash offers using photos, county records, and comparable sales data.

Do you serve rural areas of California?

Yes! We serve all 58 California counties including rural and remote areas. Virtual consultations eliminate distance barriers completely.

What documents should I have ready?

We'll email you a preparation checklist after you schedule, but generally: any foreclosure notices, recent mortgage statements, property tax information, and a rough idea of your property's condition and value.
