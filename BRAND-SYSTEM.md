## Voice & Tone

**We sound like:** A knowledgeable neighbor who happens to be a real estate expert — calm, confident, no panic
**We don't sound like:** A sleazy infomercial, a lawyer's TV ad, or a "get rich quick" guru

**Voice principles:**
- **Calm urgency:** Yes it's serious, but panicking doesn't help. We stay steady.
- **Knowledgeable:** We know California foreclosure law inside and out.
- **Empathetic:** Foreclosure is terrifying. We acknowledge the emotions.
- **Empowering:** We give homeowners tools, not dependency. "Here's what you can do."
- **Honest:** We don't promise to save every home. Some situations require selling. We say that.

**Language rules:**
- Say "homeowner" not "distressed homeowner" or "defaulting borrower"
- Say "your home" not "the property" or "the asset"
- Say "options" not "last chance" or "only hope"
- Say "free consultation" not "free quote"
- Never guarantee outcomes — "we'll explore every option" not "we'll save your home"
- Never disparage lenders — stay professional

## Content Pillars

| Pillar | What it covers | Example topics |
|--------|---------------|----------------|
| **Education** | Foreclosure process, timelines, laws, rights | CA timeline, NOD vs NOS, homeowner bill of rights |
| **Options** | Solutions available to homeowners | Loan modification, short sale, forbearance, bankruptcy, reinstatement |
| **Urgency** | Time-sensitive information, deadlines | "You have X days after NOD," auction dates, response windows |
| **Stories** | Real outcomes (when available) | Success stories, case studies, "what happened when..." |
| **Trust** | Credibility, expertise, process transparency | How we work, what to expect, who we are |

**Content mix:** 30% Education, 25% Options, 20% Urgency, 15% Trust, 10% Stories

## Carousel-Specific Rules

**Format:** 1080 × 1350px (portrait, Instagram carousel)
**Slides:** 5-7 per carousel

**Slide structure:**
1. **Hook slide** (navy bg) — Bold stat or question that stops the scroll
2. **Context slide** (light bg) — "Here's why this matters"
3. **Value slides** (alternating) — Key points, one per slide
4. **Urgency slide** (dark bg) — Timeline, deadline, or "what happens if you wait"
5. **CTA slide** (orange accent) — "Call (949) 565-5285 — Free Consultation"

---

## Banned Words & Phrases

- "Distressed" (when describing people)
- "Desperate"
- "Last chance" / "Only hope"
- "Guaranteed" results
- "We buy houses" (we're not wholesalers)
- "Cash offer" (we're not investors)
- Fear-mongering language
- "Act now or lose everything"
