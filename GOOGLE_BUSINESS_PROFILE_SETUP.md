# Google Business Profile Setup Guide

## Overview
Google Business Profile (GBP) is essential for local SEO and appearing in Google Maps searches for "foreclosure help near me" type queries.

---

## Step 1: Create or Claim Your Profile

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Click **"Add your business to Google"**
4. Enter your business name: **My Foreclosure Solution**

---

## Step 2: Choose Business Category

### Primary Category
Select: **Real Estate Consultant** or **Real Estate Agency**

### Additional Categories (add 2-3 more)
- Financial Consultant
- Mortgage Broker
- Real Estate Service

---

## Step 3: Add Service Area

Since you don't have a physical storefront clients visit:

1. Select **"I deliver goods and services to my customers"**
2. Click **"Yes"** to hide your address
3. Add service areas:
   - Los Angeles County, CA
   - Orange County, CA
   - San Diego County, CA
   - Riverside County, CA
   - San Bernardino County, CA
   - (Add all California counties you serve)

## Step 7: Verification

Google needs to verify you own/operate this business.

### Verification Options:
1. **Phone verification** (fastest) - Receive automated call with code
2. **Postcard** - Mailed to your address (5-7 days)
3. **Video verification** - Record video showing business operations

**Recommended**: Phone verification if available

---

## Step 8: Add Photos

Upload these types of photos:

### Required Photos
1. **Logo** - Your business logo (square, 250x250px minimum)
2. **Cover photo** - Professional image (1024x576px recommended)

### Recommended Photos
3. Professional headshot of yourself
4. Screenshot of virtual consultation setup
5. Before/after success story images (with permission)
6. California real estate license
7. Office/workspace photo

## Step 9: Enable Messaging

1. In GBP dashboard, go to **Messages**
2. Enable **"Accept messages"**
3. Set up auto-reply:
   ```
   Thank you for reaching out to My Foreclosure Solution! We typically respond within 1 hour during business hours. For urgent matters, please call (949) 565-5285.
   ```

---

## Step 10: Add Booking Link

Once you have your Google Calendar appointment link:

1. Go to GBP dashboard > **Bookings**
2. Add appointment booking link
3. Or use "Request a quote" feature

3. Copy the short link provided

## GBP Optimization Tips

1. **Use keywords naturally** - "foreclosure help California" in description
2. **Post regularly** - 2-3 times per week
3. **Respond to all reviews** - Even negative ones professionally
4. **Add new photos monthly** - Keeps profile fresh
5. **Enable all features** - Messaging, booking, Q&A
6. **Complete 100%** - Fill out every possible field

---

## Tracking Performance

In GBP Insights, monitor:
- Search queries used to find you
- Actions taken (calls, website visits, directions)
- Photo views
- Review count and rating
