## Voice & Tone

**We sound like:** A smart friend who bought a house last year and wants to save you from making their mistakes
**We don't sound like:** A real estate agent selling you something, a textbook, or a guru with a course

**Voice principles:**
- **Smart but accessible:** Break down complex topics without dumbing them down
- **Encouraging:** "You can do this" energy — buying a home feels impossible until someone shows you how
- **Data-driven:** Scores, percentages, benchmarks — we quantify readiness
- **Honest:** Some people aren't ready yet. We tell them that AND tell them how to get ready
- **Agent-friendly:** Agents are partners, not adversaries. We make their job easier.

**Language rules:**
- Say "buyer" or "future homeowner" not "lead" or "prospect"
- Say "readiness score" not "test" or "grade"
- Say "learn" not "take a course" or "enroll"
- Say "your agent" not "a realtor" (personalize the relationship)
- Never promise "you'll buy a house" — promise "you'll know if you're ready"

**Content mix:** 30% Education, 25% Score, 20% Process, 15% Agent, 10% Motivation

## Carousel-Specific Rules

**Format:** 1080 × 1350px (portrait, Instagram carousel)
**Slides:** 5-7 per carousel

**Slide structure:**
1. **Hook slide** (charcoal bg) — Stat or question: "78% of first-time buyers wish they knew this"
2. **Context slide** (cream bg) — Set up the knowledge gap
3. **Value slides** (alternating) — One concept per slide, clear and visual
4. **Score slide** (dark bg) — Connect the topic to homebuyer readiness
5. **CTA slide** (gold accent) — "Check your readiness score — homeiqacademy.com"

## Banned Words & Phrases

- "Course" or "curriculum" (we say "lessons" or "articles")
- "Students" (we say "buyers" or "future homeowners")
- "Guaranteed approval"
- "You NEED to buy a home" (buying isn't for everyone)
- "Real estate investing" (we're about primary homes, not investing)
- "Get rich" / "Build wealth through real estate"
- "Easy" (buying a home isn't easy — we make you prepared, not comfortable)

## Target Audience

**B2C (Buyers):**
- First-time homebuyers ages 25-40
- People curious about whether they can afford a home
- Renters who want to become homeowners but don't know where to start
- People with imperfect credit working toward homeownership

**B2B (Agents):**
- Real estate agents who want to educate and retain buyer leads
- Brokerages looking for lead nurture tools
- Loan officers who want to send pre-education to borrowers

- Starter: $199/month (agents)
- Growth: $599/month (agents)
