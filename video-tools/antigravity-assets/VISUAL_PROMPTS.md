## Quick Tips for AntiGravity

1. **Style:** Keep consistent - cinematic, professional, real estate focused
2. **Lighting:** Golden hour / warm for positive scenes, darker for warnings
3. **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (16:9 landscape)
4. **Format:** PNG preferred
5. **Mood:** Professional but emotional - this is about saving homes
